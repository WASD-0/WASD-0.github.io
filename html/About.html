About ATC

Australian Training Company (ATC) is a prominent provider of comprehensive business solutions, specializing in creating opportunities in education, employment, and training. Since its establishment in 1994, ATC has played a significant role in shaping the careers of over 20,000 apprentices, trainees, and students across Australia. ATC holds accreditation as both a Group Training Organisation (GTO) and a Registered Training Organisation (RTO).

What We Do

Value Description

Aspirational Matchmaking: We recognize and align individuals' aspirations with the right opportunities.

Our History

Founded in 1994 as a not-for-profit organization, the Australian Training Company initially focused on introducing structured training and employment opportunities for sports enthusiasts, promoting apprenticeships and traineeships as a pathway for life beyond sports careers. Over time, ATC expanded its group training operation to offer similar opportunities to a broader audience and developed innovative solutions to meet evolving business requirements. In response to growing industry demands for customized education programs, ATC became a Registered Training Organisation (RTO) in 1998. Our training operation emphasizes flexible study arrangements and fosters an inclusive learning culture accessible to all. As a Group Training Company and Registered Training Organisation, ATC has made a significant impact by providing employment and training to over 20,000 apprentices, trainees, and students across the country.
